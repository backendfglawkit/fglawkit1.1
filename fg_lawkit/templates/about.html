About

  • It all began in 2021 when a fellow intern confided in me, tears welling up, saying "I feel underconfident because of my law school tag. Unlike you neither does my college provide me with any resources nor do I have anyone to help me out."

  • We felt a strong urge to support our fellow law students, so we began sharing what we knew on YouTube

  • The overwhelming response we received on our social media inspired us to go the extra mile.
