Q. WHAT & WHY RAMBAAN DRAFTING ?

Being a first-generation lawyer myself, I have struggled with the problems and confusion that you might be facing now in your law internships. From constantly seeking assistance from either seniors/friends in drafting and research, to being hesitant in asking for help from fellow interns and associates- I've faced it all. With this experience of utter confusion and anxiety, I came up with this product. Here I try to answer a few questions or rather try to remove some of the obstacles that we all face during our internships.

  • No need to be totally dependent at the will or on the behest of your "associate" or "senior- internt for learning. Show your skills/drafting knowledge to them with rambaan drafting which help you as your own law school senior.
  • Don't let your "law school tag" define you. Equip yourself with the necessary "practical knowledge" before going to any internship.
  • Whether you're gearing up for UPSC or preparing for a career in the Judiciary, these videos are your perfect post-classroom resource to grasp the real-world intricacies of law and how it all plays out in the courtroom.
